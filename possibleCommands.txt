Possible arguments:

    - [exit] (stop program)

    - [print] (print state of the cube)

    - [number](layer of the cube from right side except of last left one)
      [RLFBUD](type of move)
      ['](if against clockwise direction)
      [number](times you want revolve)

    - [rotate][XYZ][*-]
      ('*' - clockwise direction)
      ('-' - against clockwise direction)

    - [scramble]
      (scramble cube)

Examples: 
	0R1 1U2 0B'1 0D2
	rotateX*
	rotataeY-
